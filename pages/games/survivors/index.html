Game Features

  • 5 Starting Classes - Warrior, Scout, Tank, Mage, Balanced
  • 9 Weapons with branching skill trees
  • 13 Enemy types with unique AI and attack patterns
  • Champion enemies with special affixes
  • Kill milestones with permanent rewards
  • Danger zones for risk/reward gameplay
  • 7-tier combo system with escalating bonuses
  • Multi-upgrade shop at level-up
